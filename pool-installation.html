Pool Installation in Sherman Oaks, CA

Ready to turn your backyard into a private oasis? CPR Pools offers premier pool installation in Sherman Oaks, providing custom-built swimming pools that combine high-end aesthetics with durable engineering. From the initial excavation to the final plastering, our team handles every aspect of the construction process, ensuring your new pool is built to last and perfectly suited to your California lifestyle.

What This Service Includes

Our pool installation process is a complete, turn-key solution. We manage everything from the heavy machinery to the delicate masonry work, so you can focus on planning your first pool party.

  • Custom 3D pool design and layout planning
  • Professional excavation and site preparation
  • High-strength steel reinforcement (rebar)
  • State-of-the-art plumbing and filtration systems
  • Shotcrete or gunite shell application
  • Premium tile, coping, and interior finishes

Specific Installation Components

We don't just build the shell; we install the heart of your pool. This includes high-efficiency pumps, heaters, saltwater systems, and advanced automation controls that allow you to manage your pool from your smartphone.

Common Problems We Solve

Building a pool in the San Fernando Valley comes with unique challenges. We solve common homeowner concerns such as:

  • Limited Space: We design "spools" and custom shapes for tight Sherman Oaks backyards.
  • Permit Complexity: We handle all City of Los Angeles building permits and inspections.
  • Soil Conditions: Our engineering ensures stability even in expansive clay or hillside locations.
  • Energy Waste: We install energy-efficient equipment that meets strict California standards.

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

A professionally installed pool from CPR Pools offers more than just a place to swim. The benefits include:

  • Expert Craftsmanship: 25 years of experience means your pool is built right the first time.
  • Increased Home Value: A well-designed pool is one of the best investments for a Southern California home.
  • Health & Wellness: Enjoy low-impact exercise and stress relief in the comfort of your own home.
  • Durable Construction: We use only the highest-grade materials to ensure your pool lasts for decades.

Our Process

  1. Consultation & Design: We meet at your home to discuss your vision and create a custom 3D design.
  2. Permitting & Engineering: We secure all necessary approvals from local building departments.
  3. Excavation & Steel: We dig the pool and install the structural steel cage.
  4. Plumbing & Shell: We install the plumbing lines and apply the concrete shell.
  5. Finishing Touches: We add your chosen tile, coping, and interior finish before filling the pool.

Why Choose Us in Sherman Oaks

As local experts, we understand the architectural styles and homeowner expectations in Sherman Oaks. We’ve built pools throughout the neighborhood and in nearby areas like Van Nuys and Studio City. Our reputation is built on transparency, quality, and a deep understanding of the local landscape.
