Spa Installation in Sherman Oaks, CA

Looking for the ultimate way to unwind after a long day? CPR Pools offers expert spa installation in Sherman Oaks, providing custom-built, in-ground spas that offer therapeutic benefits and luxury aesthetics. Whether you want a standalone spa or a beautiful spillover feature integrated into your existing pool, we provide the engineering and craftsmanship to create your perfect retreat.

What This Service Includes

Our spa installation process is comprehensive, focusing on both comfort and performance. We use high-end components to ensure your spa heats quickly and provides powerful massage therapy.

  • Custom in-ground spa design and layout
  • Professional excavation and structural steel work
  • High-performance jet configurations and plumbing
  • Energy-efficient heaters and filtration systems
  • Premium tile, coping, and interior finishes
  • Integrated lighting and automation controls

Specific Spa Features

We specialize in custom jet layouts tailored to your needs. From deep-tissue back jets to soothing foot massagers, we design the plumbing to provide the exact therapeutic experience you desire.

Common Problems We Solve

Installing a spa requires precise engineering to ensure proper heating and water flow. We solve common issues such as:

  • Slow Heating: Installing high-BTU heaters that get your spa to temperature in minutes.
  • Weak Jets: Engineering the plumbing for maximum pressure and therapeutic benefit.
  • Integration Challenges: Seamlessly adding a spa to an existing pool with matching materials.
  • Energy Inefficiency: Using insulated plumbing and high-efficiency equipment to lower operating costs.

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

A custom in-ground spa from CPR Pools offers numerous health and lifestyle benefits, including:

  • Stress Relief: Hydrotherapy is one of the best ways to reduce stress and improve sleep.
  • Muscle Recovery: Powerful jets help soothe sore muscles and improve circulation.
  • Year-Round Use: Unlike pools, spas can be enjoyed comfortably even during the cooler California winters.
  • Enhanced Aesthetics: A well-designed spa with a spillover feature adds a stunning visual element to your backyard.

Our Process

  1. Consultation: We discuss your therapeutic needs and design preferences.
  2. Design: We create a 3D model showing how the spa will look and function.
  3. Plumbing & Steel: We install the structural shell and complex jet manifold system.
  4. Equipment Setup: We install the heater, pump, and automation controls.
  5. Finishing: We apply the tile and interior finish to match your vision.

Why Choose Us in Sherman Oaks

CPR Pools has been building custom spas in Sherman Oaks for 25 years. We understand the local building codes and the desire for high-end, functional backyard features. Our team has completed successful spa installations in Glendale, Burbank, and throughout the San Fernando Valley. We pride ourselves on delivering spas that are not only beautiful but also engineered for peak performance.
