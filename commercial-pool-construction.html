Commercial Pool Construction in Sherman Oaks, CA

Building a commercial pool requires a level of engineering and regulatory expertise that goes far beyond residential construction. CPR Pools provides professional commercial pool construction in Sherman Oaks, specializing in high-capacity swimming pools for hotels, apartment complexes, community centers, and health clubs. We understand the strict health and safety codes required for public pools and ensure every project is built to the highest standards of durability and performance. With 25 years of experience, we are the San Fernando Valley's trusted partner for large-scale aquatic projects.

What This Service Includes

Our commercial construction process is comprehensive, focusing on high-volume filtration, heavy-duty materials, and strict code compliance. We manage the entire project from initial engineering to final health department approval.

  • Commercial-grade 3D design and architectural planning
  • Heavy-duty structural engineering and shell construction
  • High-capacity filtration and chemical automation systems
  • ADA-compliant access features (lifts, ramps, and stairs)
  • Commercial-grade tile, coping, and interior finishes
  • Safety equipment and signage installation

Specific Commercial Features

We specialize in large-scale filtration systems and advanced chemical controllers that ensure your water remains safe and clear even with high bather loads. Our designs also include energy-efficient commercial heaters and LED lighting systems.

Common Problems We Solve

Commercial pools face unique challenges related to usage and regulation. We solve common issues such as:

  • Code Compliance: Ensuring your pool meets all local, state, and federal health and safety regulations.
  • High Maintenance Costs: Installing high-efficiency commercial equipment that lowers operating expenses.
  • Structural Failures: Using heavy-duty engineering to ensure the pool shell can withstand constant use.
  • Water Safety: Implementing advanced chemical automation to prevent waterborne illnesses.

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

A professionally built commercial pool from CPR Pools provides more than just a place to swim. The benefits include:

  • Unmatched Durability: We use only the highest-grade commercial materials and equipment.
  • Regulatory Peace of Mind: We handle all health department inspections and approvals.
  • Enhanced Property Value: A high-quality pool is a major amenity for any commercial property.
  • Lower Long-Term Costs: Efficient systems and durable construction reduce future repair and energy bills.

Our Process

  1. Pre-Construction: We review your architectural plans and provide a detailed cost analysis.
  2. Engineering & Permitting: We secure all necessary approvals from building and health departments.
  3. Construction: Our expert team manages the entire build, focusing on structural integrity and code compliance.
  4. Commissioning: We test all systems and ensure the pool meets all health department standards.
  5. Training: We teach your facility staff how to operate and maintain the new pool systems.

We’ve completed successful projects in Burbank, Glendale, and Woodland Hills. Our reputation is built on technical excellence, regulatory expertise, and a commitment to delivering projects on time and within budget.
