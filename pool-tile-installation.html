Pool Tile Installation in Sherman Oaks, CA

Is your pool tile cracked, falling off, or covered in white calcium deposits? CPR Pools provides professional pool tile installation in Sherman Oaks, offering a wide selection of high-quality glass, ceramic, and porcelain tiles to refresh your pool's look. New tile is one of the most cost-effective ways to modernize your swimming pool and protect the waterline from staining and erosion. With 25 years of masonry experience, we ensure every tile is perfectly aligned and securely bonded.

What This Service Includes

Our tile installation process is meticulous, focusing on both aesthetic precision and long-term durability. We use specialized pool-grade mortars and grouts that withstand constant chemical exposure.

  • Removal of old, damaged tile and mortar
  • Surface preparation and leveling of the waterline beam
  • Professional installation of ceramic, porcelain, or glass tile
  • Precision cutting for steps, corners, and complex shapes
  • High-performance, waterproof grouting
  • Final cleaning and sealing of the tile line

Specific Tile Options

We offer everything from classic 6x6 blue ceramic tiles to modern, shimmering glass mosaics. Glass tile, in particular, is a popular choice in Sherman Oaks for its ability to reflect light and create a luxury, resort-like feel.

Common Problems We Solve

Pool tile is subject to harsh conditions that can lead to failure. We solve common issues such as:

  • Falling Tiles: Fixing areas where tiles have popped off due to beam movement or poor bonding.
  • Calcium Buildup: Replacing tiles that are permanently stained by hard water deposits.
  • Cracked Tiles: Removing tiles that have cracked due to structural shifting or impact.
  • Outdated Styles: Modernizing pools with contemporary colors and textures.

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

New tile from CPR Pools provides more than just a visual upgrade. The benefits include:

  • Stunning Aesthetics: Instantly transform the look of your pool with vibrant colors and patterns.
  • Waterline Protection: Tile protects the concrete shell from the "scum line" and chemical erosion.
  • Increased Durability: We use high-grade materials that are specifically designed for submerged environments.
  • Easier Cleaning: Smooth, modern tiles are much easier to scrub and maintain than old, pitted ceramic.

Our Process

  1. Material Selection: We help you choose the perfect tile from our extensive catalog.
  2. Preparation: We carefully remove the old tile and grind the surface to ensure a perfect bond.
  3. Layout: We plan the tile placement to minimize cuts and ensure a symmetrical look.
  4. Installation: Our master masons set each tile by hand for a level, professional finish.
  5. Grouting: We apply a waterproof grout that matches your tile and resists staining.

Why Choose Us in Sherman Oaks

CPR Pools has been the leader in pool tile installation in Sherman Oaks for over two decades. We understand the local water conditions that cause calcium buildup and the soil movement that can lead to cracked tile lines. Our team has completed hundreds of tile projects in Van Nuys, Valley Village, and North Hollywood, earning a reputation for craftsmanship and attention to detail.
