Pool Finishing in Sherman Oaks, CA

The interior finish is what gives your pool its color, texture, and durability. CPR Pools provides expert pool finishing in Sherman Oaks, offering a wide range of high-performance materials to complete your new construction or remodeling project. We specialize in hand-troweled plaster, pebble, and quartz finishes that are designed to withstand the unique climate and water conditions of the San Fernando Valley. With 25 years of experience, we ensure every finish is applied with precision for a smooth, long-lasting result.

What This Service Includes

Our pool finishing process is the final, critical step in the construction journey. We use specialized materials and techniques to ensure a waterproof, beautiful interior.

  • Surface preparation and bond coat application
  • Professional hand-troweling of the chosen finish
  • Material selection (Plaster, Pebble, or Quartz)
  • Acid wash and final surface cleaning
  • Initial water balancing and chemical start-up
  • Detailed care instructions for the new finish

Specific Finishing Options

We offer a variety of finishes to fit your aesthetic and budget. From classic white plaster to vibrant pebble finishes with shimmering glass beads, we help you choose the material that will make your water look its best.

Common Problems We Solve

A poorly applied finish can lead to staining, peeling, and a rough surface. We solve common finishing issues such as:

  • Rough Textures: Ensuring a smooth, comfortable finish for swimmers.
  • Premature Wear: Using high-grade materials that resist chemical erosion.
  • Uneven Coloring: Applying the finish with expert precision to avoid mottling and streaks.
  • Poor Bonding: Ensuring the finish adheres perfectly to the concrete shell.

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

A professional pool finish from CPR Pools provides more than just a beautiful look. The benefits include:

  • Enhanced Durability: Modern finishes are designed to last significantly longer than traditional plaster.
  • Stunning Water Color: Choose a finish that creates the exact shade of blue you desire.
  • Improved Comfort: A smooth, well-applied finish is kinder to skin and swimwear.
  • Easier Maintenance: High-quality finishes are more resistant to algae and staining.

Our Process

  1. Material Consultation: We help you choose the best finish for your style and budget.
  2. Preparation: We ensure the concrete shell is clean and ready for the bond coat.
  3. Application: Our master finishers apply the material by hand for a perfect result.
  4. Curing: We manage the initial water fill and chemical treatment to ensure the finish cures correctly.
  5. Final Inspection: We perform a detailed walkthrough to ensure the finish meets our high standards.

Why Choose Us in Sherman Oaks

CPR Pools has been the leader in pool finishing in Sherman Oaks for over two decades. We understand the local water chemistry and the climate conditions that affect how pool finishes cure and age. Our team has completed hundreds of finishing projects in Studio City, Encino, and throughout the San Fernando Valley, earning a reputation for quality and reliability.
