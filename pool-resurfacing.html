Pool Resurfacing in Sherman Oaks, CA

Is your pool surface rough, stained, or starting to peel? CPR Pools provides professional pool resurfacing in Sherman Oaks to restore the smooth, beautiful finish your swimming pool deserves. A fresh surface doesn't just look better—it protects the structural integrity of your pool and makes it more comfortable for swimmers. With 25 years of experience, we offer the highest quality plaster, pebble, and quartz finishes available in Southern California.

What This Service Includes

Our resurfacing process is thorough and designed for longevity. We don't just "paint over" the problem; we strip away the old material and apply a brand-new, durable finish.

  • Draining and safe disposal of pool water
  • Removal of old plaster and surface prep (chip-out)
  • Structural inspection and minor crack repair
  • Application of high-quality bond coat
  • Professional hand-troweled finish (Plaster, Pebble, or Quartz)
  • Acid wash and initial water balancing

Specific Resurfacing Options

We offer a variety of finishes to fit your budget and style. Choose from classic white plaster for a traditional look, or upgrade to pebble finishes for a natural, textured feel that lasts significantly longer and resists staining.

Common Problems We Solve

Over time, pool surfaces naturally degrade due to chemical exposure and UV rays. We solve common issues such as:

  • Rough Surfaces: Ending the "scraped knees" caused by pitted plaster.
  • Staining & Discoloration: Removing unsightly mineral deposits and organic stains.
  • Delamination: Fixing areas where the plaster is peeling away from the shell.
  • Water Loss: Sealing hairline cracks that cause slow but constant leaks.

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

Resurfacing your pool is one of the most effective ways to modernize your backyard. The benefits include:

  • Improved Comfort: A smooth surface is much kinder to skin and swimwear.
  • Easier Maintenance: Modern finishes are more resistant to algae and chemical imbalances.
  • Stunning Aesthetics: Choose from vibrant colors and textures that make your water sparkle.
  • Extended Pool Life: A new surface acts as a waterproof barrier for your pool's concrete shell.

Our Process

  1. Surface Assessment: We inspect your pool to determine the best resurfacing material.
  2. Preparation: We drain the pool and remove the old, damaged surface layer.
  3. Bonding: We apply a specialized coat to ensure the new finish sticks perfectly.
  4. Application: Our expert finishers apply the new material by hand for a smooth result.
  5. Refill & Start-up: We fill the pool and provide the initial chemical treatment to cure the surface.
