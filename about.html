About CPR Pools

Building dreams and creating backyard paradises for over 25 years.

Our Story

Founded by Robert, CPR Pools began with a simple vision: to provide California homeowners with pool services that combine artistic design with structural integrity. Over the past two and a half decades, we have grown from a small local team into one of the most respected pool building companies in the region.

We believe that a swimming pool is more than just a place to swim—it's the centerpiece of your home's outdoor life. That's why we treat every project with the same level of dedication, whether it's a minor tile repair or a full-scale custom construction.

Meet Robert

With a background in engineering and a passion for landscape design, Robert oversees every project to ensure it meets the high standards CPR Pools is known for. His hands-on approach and eye for detail are what set our work apart.

"We don't just build pools; we build relationships."
