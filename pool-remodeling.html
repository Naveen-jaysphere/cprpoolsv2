Pool Remodeling in Sherman Oaks, CA

If your backyard pool looks like it belongs in another decade, it’s time for a professional upgrade. CPR Pools provides expert pool remodeling in Sherman Oaks, helping homeowners transform outdated swimming pools into modern, energy-efficient retreats. Whether you want to change the shape of your pool, add a spa, or simply refresh the aesthetic, our team delivers high-end results with 25 years of local experience.

What This Service Includes

Our pool remodeling services are comprehensive, covering everything from structural changes to aesthetic finishing touches. We work closely with you to ensure your renovated pool matches your vision and lifestyle.

  • Complete structural redesign and reshaping
  • Depth adjustments and tanning ledge additions
  • Water feature installations (waterfalls, deck jets, bubblers)
  • Energy-efficient equipment upgrades
  • LED lighting system installation

Specific Remodeling Components

We handle every detail of the renovation process, including plumbing, electrical, and masonry work. Our goal is to improve both the beauty and the functionality of your pool, ensuring it runs better than the day it was built.

Common Problems We Solve

Many homeowners in Sherman Oaks deal with aging pools that are either leaking, inefficient, or simply an eyesore. We solve common pain points such as:

  • Cracked shells and structural leaks
  • Outdated, rough plaster that scrapes skin
  • High energy bills from old, inefficient pumps
  • Broken or missing tiles and coping
  • Dull, poorly lit pool areas that feel unsafe at night

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

Investing in a pool remodel doesn't just make your backyard look better—it adds real value to your home and saves you money in the long run. By choosing CPR Pools, you get:

  • Increased Property Value: A modern pool is a major selling point for California homes.
  • Lower Utility Costs: New variable-speed pumps and LED lights use significantly less power.
  • Enhanced Safety: Modern surfaces and lighting make your pool area safer for family and guests.
  • Personalized Oasis: Tailor your pool to exactly how you use it, whether for exercise or entertaining.

Our Process

  1. On-Site Consultation: We inspect your current pool and discuss your goals and budget.
  2. Design & Planning: We create a detailed plan for the structural and aesthetic changes.
  3. Demolition & Construction: Our crew carefully removes old materials and begins the transformation.
  4. Finishing & Equipment: We install new surfaces, tiles, and energy-efficient systems.
  5. Final Inspection: We test everything to ensure your new pool is perfect and ready for use.
