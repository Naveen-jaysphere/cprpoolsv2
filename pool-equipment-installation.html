Pool Equipment Installation in Sherman Oaks, CA

Your pool equipment is the heart and lungs of your swimming pool. CPR Pools provides professional pool equipment installation in Sherman Oaks, specializing in high-efficiency systems that keep your water clean, clear, and comfortable while lowering your energy bills. From variable-speed pumps to advanced saltwater systems and smart automation, we install the latest technology from the industry's leading brands. With 25 years of experience, we ensure your equipment is sized correctly and installed for peak performance.

What This Service Includes

Our equipment installation process is thorough, focusing on both performance and ease of use. We don't just swap out parts; we optimize your entire filtration and heating system.

  • Installation of energy-efficient variable-speed pumps
  • High-performance gas and electric pool heaters
  • Advanced cartridge and D.E. filtration systems
  • Saltwater chlorine generators and chemical feeders
  • Smart pool automation and smartphone controls
  • LED color-changing pool and spa lighting

Specific Equipment Components

We specialize in installing automation systems that allow you to control your pool's temperature, lighting, and water features from your phone. This makes managing your pool effortless and ensures it's always ready when you are.

Common Problems We Solve

Old or poorly installed equipment can lead to high energy bills and cloudy water. We solve common issues such as:

  • High Energy Costs: Replacing old single-speed pumps with variable-speed models that save up to 90% on energy.
  • Cloudy Water: Installing correctly sized filters and high-efficiency pumps for better circulation.
  • Slow Heating: Installing high-BTU heaters that get your pool or spa to temperature quickly.
  • Complex Operation: Simplifying your pool's management with intuitive automation systems.

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

Professional equipment installation from CPR Pools provides more than just clean water. The benefits include:

  • Significant Energy Savings: Modern equipment is designed to be highly efficient, paying for itself over time.
  • Crystal Clear Water: Better filtration and circulation mean a cleaner, healthier swimming environment.
  • Effortless Management: Control your entire pool system with the touch of a button.
  • Quieter Operation: New variable-speed pumps are significantly quieter than older models.

Our Process

  1. Equipment Audit: We assess your current system and identify areas for improvement.
  2. Recommendation: We suggest the best equipment for your pool's size and your lifestyle.
  3. Installation: Our expert technicians install the new equipment and optimize the plumbing.
  4. Programming: We set up your automation and teach you how to use the new system.
  5. Follow-up: We ensure everything is running perfectly and answer any questions you have.
