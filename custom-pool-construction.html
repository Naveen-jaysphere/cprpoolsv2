Custom Pool Construction in Sherman Oaks, CA

Why settle for a standard pool when you can have a bespoke masterpiece? CPR Pools provides high-end custom pool construction in Sherman Oaks, specializing in unique, one-of-a-kind designs that reflect your personal style and enhance your property’s architecture. We combine 25 years of engineering expertise with creative design to build pools that are as functional as they are beautiful.

What This Service Includes

Our custom construction process is a collaborative journey. We handle every detail, from the first conceptual sketch to the final water balancing, ensuring a seamless experience.

  • Bespoke 3D design and architectural planning
  • Custom shapes, infinity edges, and zero-entry features
  • Integrated spas, waterfalls, and fire features
  • Advanced LED lighting and smart automation systems
  • Premium material selection (Natural stone, glass tile, pebble finishes)
  • Energy-efficient equipment and filtration systems

Specific Custom Features

We specialize in the "wow" factor. This includes sunken fire pits, swim-up bars, tanning ledges, and complex water features that turn your backyard into a luxury resort. If you can dream it, we can build it.

Common Problems We Solve

Standard pool builders often struggle with complex designs or difficult sites. We solve challenges such as:

  • Complex Terrain: Building stable, beautiful pools on hillsides or uneven lots.
  • Unique Visions: Translating your specific aesthetic goals into a structural reality.
  • Integration Issues: Ensuring your pool perfectly complements your home’s existing architecture.
  • High Maintenance: Installing advanced automation that makes managing a custom pool effortless.

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

A custom pool from CPR Pools is more than just a place to swim; it's a lifestyle upgrade. The benefits include:

  • Unmatched Quality: We use only the finest materials and most skilled craftsmen.
  • Personalized Design: Your pool will be unique to your home and your needs.
  • Resort-Style Living: Enjoy the luxury of a high-end hotel in your own backyard.
  • Long-Term Value: A custom, well-built pool significantly increases the desirability and value of your home.

Our Process

  1. Discovery: We discuss your vision, budget, and how you plan to use the space.
  2. Design: Our designers create a detailed 3D model of your custom pool.
  3. Engineering: We develop structural plans that meet all local safety and building codes.
  4. Construction: Our team manages the entire build, from excavation to the final tile work.
  5. Commissioning: We teach you how to use your new pool’s features and automation systems.

We’ve built award-winning pools in Calabasas, Toluca Lake, and Burbank. Our commitment to quality and our ability to handle complex engineering makes us the San Fernando Valley's premier custom pool builder.
