Pool Crack Repair in Sherman Oaks, CA

A crack in your pool is more than just an eyesore—it’s a threat to your home’s foundation and your water bill. CPR Pools specializes in permanent pool crack repair in Sherman Oaks, using advanced structural stabilization techniques to fix leaks and prevent further damage. We don't just patch the surface; we address the underlying structural issues to ensure your pool remains watertight and safe for years to come.

What This Service Includes

Our crack repair service is a multi-step process that ensures a permanent fix. We use the same high-end materials and techniques used in large-scale commercial pool engineering.

  • Leak detection and crack assessment
  • Structural stapling using carbon fiber or torque-lock staples
  • Epoxy or polyurethane resin injection
  • Surface preparation and plaster matching
  • Waterproofing and sealing of the repaired area

Specific Repair Components

We utilize specialized tools to "stitch" the concrete back together. This prevents the crack from expanding due to soil movement or temperature changes, which is a common problem in the San Fernando Valley.

Common Problems We Solve

Pool cracks are often caused by shifting soil or poor initial construction. We solve critical issues such as:

  • Structural Cracks: Deep fissures that go through the concrete shell.
  • Surface Crazing: Small, spider-web cracks in the plaster.
  • Tile Line Cracks: Separations where the pool meets the coping.
  • Water Loss: Stopping the constant leak that wastes thousands of gallons of water.

Benefits of Choosing Our Service

Addressing pool cracks early can save you tens of thousands of dollars in future repairs. The benefits of our professional repair include:

  • Permanent Solutions: Our structural staples prevent cracks from ever reopening.
  • Foundation Protection: Stopping leaks prevents water from undermining your pool or home foundation.
  • Lower Bills: A watertight pool means you stop paying for water that just leaks into the ground.
  • Peace of Mind: Know that your pool is structurally sound and safe for your family.

Our Process

  1. Inspection: We identify the type and cause of the crack.
  2. Preparation: We grind out the crack to expose the structural concrete.
  3. Stapling: We install high-tension staples across the crack to hold the shell together.
  4. Injection: We fill the void with specialized structural epoxy.
  5. Finishing: We patch the plaster and tile to match your pool's existing aesthetic.

With 25 years of experience in Sherman Oaks, we’ve seen every type of pool failure imaginable. We understand the local soil conditions in the Valley that lead to shifting and cracking. Our team has successfully repaired pools in Tarzana, Woodland Hills, and throughout the San Fernando Valley, providing a level of expertise that general contractors simply can't match.
